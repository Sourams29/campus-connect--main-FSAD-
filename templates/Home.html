Frequently Asked Questions

How can I register for an event?

You can register for any event by visiting the event listing page and clicking on the "Register" button for the respective event. Make sure you're logged in to your account.

What is the purpose of the Event Management System?

The Event Management System allows users to browse, register, and manage events conveniently. It is designed to streamline event organization and participation for students, faculty, and external organizers.

How can I host an event on this platform?

To host an event, navigate to the "Event Manager" section, fill out the event form with the necessary details, and submit it for approval by the admin. Once approved, your event will be listed on the platform.

Can I view the events I have registered for?

Yes, you can view all your registered events under the "My Events" section in your dashboard. This section lists details of each event you've registered for.

What happens if an event I registered for gets cancelled?

If an event is canceled, you will receive a notification through email and your dashboard. Any registration fees paid (if applicable) will be refunded as per the platform's policies.

Is there a way to contact the event organizers?

Yes, you can contact the event organizers by visiting the event details page and using the "Contact Organizer" option, which provides their email address or a messaging form.
